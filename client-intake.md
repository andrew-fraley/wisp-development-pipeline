**Client Onboarding Questionnaire**

NOTE: This is a basic overview of my client intake questionnaire used to intake a client and get an in-depth understanding of their current security posture, business operaitons, and data handling practices.
**Sections:**

**1. Client Bussiness Overview**
- Covers the business information, contact information, bursiness structure, number of employees, and other information pertaining to the business.

**2. Data and Asset Inventory**
- This is where relevant information pertaining to the types of data the client handles, how they handle it in their current business operations, third-party vendors used and the types of digital and/or physical assets attached to busines operations.
- Additionally, I create a data flow map of the client's data handling process, this gives a picture of how the organization goes about collecting, processing, storing, sharing and disposing of data. (tools, software, platforms, etc.)

**3. Compliance and Regulatory Requirements**
- What regulatory standards is the client required to comply with, are they currently in compliance, have they had auditing or assessments conducted to validate compliance.
- Covers the current security documentation and practices in place dealing with compliance, does the client have an IRS WISP (if so when was the last review of the WISP).
- Has the client had any data security incidents occur in their organization and details pertaining to these possible incidents.

**4. Current Security Posture**
- Questions the client on their current data security practices, security tools in place, incident response plan/playbook, access controls, and security awareness training practices for client's personnel.

**5. Goals and Concerns**
- This section covers the goals of the information security consultation contract, compliance goals, exact servics requesting from security contractor, and any concerns the client has for the project and the contractor.

**6. Communication and Collaboration**
- Details on how the contractor and client will conduct collaboration on the project.  

**Any additional information required for the project.**

Note: This questionnaire is accompanied by a client intake consultatoin for the information security consultant to get a full picture of the client's organization and all relevant details to conduct all security assessments/audits pertaininng to the requested services of the proejct.
