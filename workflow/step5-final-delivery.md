**Final Delivery**

**Overview**

This step is completed after the three deliverable documents are published following the implementation guide step and consultation is concluded. Post implementation step, any edits that need to be made to the documents are finalized and reviewed during this final delivery. After the edits are made and the docuemnts reviewed, the three deliverable documents are published and delivered to the client. 

Note: Any edits to the security plan should have been discovered and handled during the implementation consultation. This step is just editing the document drafts following the implementation step and finalizing them in the final documents to be published for the deliverables. 

**Documents**

1. Security Audit Findings
2. IRS Written Information Security Plan
3. Implementation Strategy Guide

**Conclusion**

The reason for these three documents is because this allows for a clean product stack for the purpose of a deliverable WISP project for a client. 

- Security Audit Findings document is the justification for the customized security plan.
- WISP document is the core compliance document and information security plan for the client's practice to follow for compliance and to maintain a secure business operation.
- Implementation Guide document is the actionable steps the client should take to put the policies and procedures of the security plan into practice.


Additional Notes: As an information security contractor I provide packagable "products" with different levels of complexity based on the client's project need and the scope of the contract. For instance, your WISP needs a security assessment and yearly reviews, these can vary in scope based on the client's scope and their organizations details (size, services they provide, etc.). Also, a proper WISP should include an incident response plan, this is something that van widely vary in scope based on the factors mentioned above, so for a small or solo tax practice, an incident response plan may be a simple attachment docuement on the WISP or a full IR Playbook. This repo does not fully cover every variable detail included in the development of a WISP and I plan to add more repos to my Github showcasing the process for things like IR Playbook development and Risk Assessments.
